# Image generation notes

All four images were generated with the built-in image generation tool for this project, then optimized to WebP.

## Hero — `hero-wetland.webp`

Ultra-wide cinematic aerial view of a futuristic but believable horticultural expo sensitively integrated into Nong Dae wetlands, with lotus waterways, Isan-inspired contemporary pavilions, elevated walks and dawn light. Palette: deep navy, emerald, teal, lotus pink and restrained gold. No text, logos or fantasy-scale plants.

## Orchid Cloud Garden — `garden-orchid.webp`

Luxury botanical editorial photograph of a curved glass pavilion beside a wetland pool, filled with Thai orchids, fine mist and subtle Isan-pattern screens. Soft early-morning shafts of light. No text or logos.

## Living Wetland — `garden-wetland.webp`

Conservation-focused landscape photograph of a timber boardwalk winding through lotus ponds, reeds and contemporary Isan birdwatching pavilions after rain. Fresh sunrise, realistic ecology and a calm atmosphere. No text or logos.

## Garden of Light — `garden-light.webp`

Refined night-event photograph of a botanical wetland garden with a curved pavilion, reflective water, restrained firefly-like illumination and subtle volumetric beams. Deep navy, emerald and warm gold. No neon overload, text or logos.
